# Patient Profile — User Guide

## Overview
The Patient Profile page lets you manage your personal information and health details. Doctors can see your health info during a consultation to provide better care.

## Accessing your profile
After logging in as a patient, click your avatar or name in the top-right corner → **"My Profile"**, or navigate directly to `/patient/profile`.

## Profile Picture
- Click your avatar circle or the **"Change photo"** button.
- Select a JPEG, PNG, WebP, or GIF image under 5 MB.
- A preview appears immediately while the image uploads to the cloud.
- Your new photo is shown everywhere in the app after upload.

## Personal Information tab
| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Full Name** | Your display name shown to doctors |
| **Phone Number** | Contact number (optional) |

Click **"Save changes"** after editing. A confirmation toast appears at the bottom of the screen.

## Health Information tab
This information is shared with doctors during consultations.

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Date of Birth** | Used for age-related medical context |
| **Blood Type** | Useful in emergencies |
| **Weight (kg)** | Helps with medication dosing |
| **Height (cm)** | Used for BMI and growth tracking |
| **Allergies** | Enter multiple values separated by commas (e.g. `Penicillin, Peanuts`) |
| **Current Medications** | List what you currently take (e.g. `Metformin 500mg`) |
| **Medical History** | Free-text notes on past surgeries, hospitalizations, or chronic conditions |

Click **"Save health info"** to save. All fields are optional — fill in as much as you know.

## What doctors can see
Doctors can see your health information when you book an appointment with them. The information is visible in the consultation room and in your medical record.

## What you cannot change
- Your **email address** is permanent (set at registration).
- Your **role** (Patient) cannot be changed.
- Your **password** cannot be changed through the profile page yet — contact support.
