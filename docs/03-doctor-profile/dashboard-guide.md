# Dashboards & Navigation — User Guide

## Patient Dashboard

The patient dashboard is the first screen you see after logging in. It shows a snapshot of your health activity.

### Sections

**Hello greeting** — Displays your first name and today's date context.

**Quick stats row** (three cards):
- **Upcoming appointments** — count of confirmed/pending appointments in the future
- **Unread notifications** — count of unread messages
- **Find doctors CTA** — quick link to the doctor search page

**Upcoming Appointments** — A list of your next three appointments, each showing:
- Doctor's name
- Appointment date, day of week, and time
- Status badge (pending / confirmed)

If you have no upcoming appointments, a prompt links to the Find Doctors page.

**View all** link in the top-right of the appointments card navigates to the full appointments list.

---

## Doctor Dashboard

The doctor dashboard shows your daily schedule and patient workload at a glance.

### Sections

**Greeting** — Displays "Dr. [FirstName]" and today's context.

**Quick stats row**:
- **Today's appointments** — confirmed/pending appointments scheduled for today
- **Pending requests** — total pending appointments (from any date) awaiting confirmation
- **Total patients** — unique patients who have had confirmed or completed appointments with you

**Today's Schedule** — Up to five of today's appointments, showing:
- Patient name
- Appointment time and duration
- Status badge

**View all appointments** link navigates to your full appointment list.

**Profile not set up notice** — If you haven't completed your doctor profile, the dashboard shows a prompt to do so. You won't appear in patient search until your profile is complete.

## Navigation Sidebar

Both roles have a persistent sidebar on the left (desktop only; a mobile menu is on the roadmap).

### Patient sidebar items
| Item | Where it goes |
|---|---|
| Dashboard | Your health overview |
| Find Doctors | Browse and search for specialists |
| Appointments | All your appointments (upcoming, past, cancelled) |
| Medical Records | Your consultation history and prescriptions |
| Notifications | All past notifications |
| My Profile | Personal info, health info, avatar |

### Doctor sidebar items
| Item | Where it goes |
|---|---|
| Dashboard | Your schedule overview |
| Appointments | All appointments (pending, confirmed, etc.) |
| Patients | Unique patient list |
| Consultation Notes | All medical records you've created |
| Notifications | All past notifications |
| My Profile | Professional info and availability slots |

### User footer
The bottom of the sidebar shows your avatar, name, and email. The **Sign out** button logs you out and redirects to the login page.
