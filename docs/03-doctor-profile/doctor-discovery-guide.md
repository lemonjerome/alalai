# Doctor Discovery — User Guide

## Who This Is For
Patients who want to find and book a doctor through AlalAI.

---

## How to Browse Doctors

From the left sidebar, click **Find Doctors**. The discovery page shows a grid of verified, accepting doctors.

### Filters (left sidebar)
| Filter | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Search** | Full-text search across doctor names, bios, and specializations. Press Enter or click away to apply. |
| **Specialization** | Narrow results to one specialty (e.g., Cardiology, Pediatrics) |
| **Available On** | Only show doctors who have open slots on the selected date |
| **Clear filters** | Resets all filters at once |

> All filter state is stored in the URL — you can share or bookmark a filtered view and the Back button works correctly.

---

## Reading a Doctor Card

Each card shows:
- **Name** — with a blue ✓ badge if the doctor is verified
- **Specializations** — up to two, with a "+N" badge for more
- **Bio** — first two lines (hover or click to see full)
- **Rating** — star average and review count
- **Consultation fee** — per session in Philippine Peso
- **Years of experience**
- **Next available** — if you used the "Available On" filter, the time of their next open slot that day

Click **View Profile & Book** to open the full doctor profile.

---

## Doctor Detail Page

The detail page shows:
- Full bio and all specializations
- Complete education history
- License number and verification status
- Rating and review count
- Consultation fee

### Booking Calendar

Below the profile, an interactive **monthly calendar** highlights dates that have open slots:
- Grayed-out dates are fully booked or no availability set
- Click a highlighted date to see available time slots for that day
- Click a time slot to start booking

Clicking a time slot takes you directly to the booking wizard with that date and time pre-selected.

---

## Pagination

Results show 12 doctors per page. Use **Previous** / **Next** at the bottom of the grid to navigate.

---

## Doctor Is Not Accepting Patients
Some profiles display "This doctor is not currently accepting new appointments." This means the doctor has temporarily turned off new bookings — their profile is visible but the booking calendar is hidden.

---

## Tips for Finding the Right Doctor
- Use the **Specialization** filter if you know what type of doctor you need
- Use **Available On** to immediately see who has slots on your preferred date
- Check the **bio** and **education** for background before booking
- The **rating** reflects feedback from past patients
