# AI Doctor Recommendation — User Guide

## What Is "Find by Symptoms"?

"Find by Symptoms" is an AI-powered tool in the Patient sidebar that listens to what you describe in plain language, reasons about the most appropriate type of specialist, and immediately shows you available doctors you can book with one click.

---

## How to Use It

### 1. Navigate to Find by Symptoms
In the patient sidebar, click **Find by Symptoms** (with the ✨ icon). The page is available any time you are logged in as a patient.

### 2. Describe Your Symptoms
Type freely in the text box. There is no list to pick from — just say what is bothering you. Be as specific as you like:

> *"I've had sharp chest pain and shortness of breath for two days, especially when lying down."*
> *"My 4-year-old has had a fever of 39°C for two days and won't eat."*
> *"I notice a spreading red rash on my arms with intense itching after being outside."*

- **Minimum**: 10 characters  
- **Maximum**: 1,000 characters  
- The more detail you provide, the better the AI can reason about your condition.

### 3. Click "Get AI Recommendation"
The button activates once you have typed at least 10 characters. While the AI thinks, you will see an animated skeleton loading state.

### 4. Read the AI's Reasoning
A blue card appears with the AI's explanation of *why* a particular specialist is recommended. This is a supportive, empathetic message — not a diagnosis.

### 5. Browse and Book
Below the reasoning, you see a list of available doctors in the recommended specialization. Each card shows:
- Doctor name and verified badge (✓)
- Star rating and review count
- Consultation fee in Philippine Peso (₱)
- Years of experience
- A short bio excerpt

Click **Book** on any card to go straight to that doctor's profile and appointment calendar.

### 6. Try Again
If you want to describe different symptoms, click **"Describe different symptoms"** at the bottom to reset the form.

---

## Supported Specializations

The AI routes to exactly one of these five areas:

| Specialization | Typical conditions |
|---|---|
| **General Practitioner** | Fever, fatigue, colds, routine check-ups, unclear symptoms |
| **Pediatrics** | Any condition in infants, children, and adolescents |
| **Dermatology** | Skin rashes, acne, hair loss, eczema, psoriasis |
| **Mental Health** | Anxiety, depression, stress, sleep disorders, mood changes |
| **Cardiology** | Chest pain, palpitations, shortness of breath, high blood pressure |

---

## What Happens If No Doctors Are Found?

If no doctors with the recommended specialization are currently accepting patients, you will see a prompt to browse all doctors. You can also return to the main Doctors page and filter manually.

---

## Important Disclaimer

> This tool provides **general guidance only** and is **not a substitute for professional medical advice**. The AI cannot examine you, view your medical history, or diagnose conditions. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional.  
> For life-threatening emergencies, **call 911** or go to the nearest emergency room immediately.
