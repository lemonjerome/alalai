# Notifications — User Guide

## What Are Notifications?
Notifications are real-time messages that keep you informed about activity related to your appointments and medical records. They appear in the notification bell in the sidebar and arrive instantly without needing to refresh the page.

---

## Notification Types

| Type | When You Receive It |
|---|---|
| **Appointment Confirmed** | When a booking is successfully made (both patient and doctor) |
| **Appointment Cancelled** | When either party cancels an appointment |
| **Appointment Rescheduled** | When a patient reschedules (both parties notified) |
| **Appointment Reminder** | 24 hours before a scheduled appointment |
| **Medical Record Available** | When the doctor adds consultation notes after a session |

---

## The Notification Bell

The bell icon in the navigation sidebar shows an unread count badge when you have new notifications. Click the bell to open the notification panel:

- **Blue dot** — unread notification
- **Time stamp** — how long ago it was sent (e.g., "2h ago")
- **Click to read** — clicking a notification marks it as read
- **Mark all read** — clears the unread badge for all notifications at once
- **View all** — opens the full notifications history page

---

## Full Notifications Page

Click **Notifications** in the sidebar (or "View all" from the bell panel) to see your full notification history, newest first. Use the **Mark all read** button to clear all unread indicators.

---

## Real-time Updates
Notifications arrive in real-time without refreshing. When a new notification comes in:
1. The bell badge count increments immediately
2. A toast notification appears briefly in the corner (if you are on another page)
3. The notification appears at the top of your list

---

## 24-Hour Appointment Reminders
The system automatically sends a reminder for every upcoming appointment approximately 24 hours in advance. You do not need to do anything — reminders arrive automatically.
